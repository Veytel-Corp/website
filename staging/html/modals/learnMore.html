VeyTel™

VeyTel™ is a woman-owned Small Business. We are Physicians, AI Engineers, Data Scientists, Electrical Engineers, Computer Scientists, and Business Leaders. We are dedicated to creating technology that helps clinicians and pharmaceuticals provide high quality care to their patients in a more efficient and streamlined manner. How we accomplish this... we provide state of the art AI software to assist clinicians and pharmaceutical companies with accurate treatment plans and clinical trials. The benefits of our software will result in reduced hospital stays and shorter trials. This will drastically lower patient, hospital and insurance costs for respiratory illnesses and melanoma.
