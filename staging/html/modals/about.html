About Us

VeyTel is a woman-owned small business, which consists of professionals including physicians and AI engineers. We provide advanced AI software to assist clinicians and pharmaceutical companies with accurate treatment plans and clinical trials, aiming to significantly lower costs for respiratory illnesses and melanoma for patients, hospitals, and insurers.
